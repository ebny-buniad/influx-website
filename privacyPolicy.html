Privacy Policy

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?
We gather personal data that you give it to us. When you engage in activities on the Services, when you contact us in any other way, or when you indicate an interest in learning more about us or our products and services, we gather personal information that you voluntarily disclose to us.

Personal Data You Have Provided. The context of your interactions with us and the Services, the decisions you make, and the goods and features you use all influence the personal information we gather. The following types of personal data may be included in our collection:

  1. Your name;
  2. Your email address;
  3. Your country of residence;
  4. Your activity;
  5. Information relating to the browser, device and operating system with which you accessed the company's website;
  6. Recordings of calls made to our customer service
  7. Other information you provide to us.
We automatically collect certain information when you visit, use, or navigate the Services.
2. HOW DO WE USE THIS INFORMATION AND WHAT IS THE LEGAL BASIS FOR USE?
We handle the personal data mentioned in paragraph 1 for the following reasons:

  1. To send you our periodic newsletter or other updates about new products or promotional offers;
  2. To track and analyze the usage of our websites and online services. This helps us to monitor, improve, and protect our products, content, services, and websites, both online and offline, based on our legitimate interests;
  3. To publish images in which you appear on our internet and social media channels (such as Facebook, Instagram) as well as on traditional print and broadcast media (radio, television, etc.).
When necessary, we process your personal data based on the consent you have provided, which you can change or withdraw by contacting us at info@influxbn.com. If you are already a customer, we may use your data for communications based on our legitimate interest. However, you have the right to stop receiving any commercial communications from us that are not directly related to your existing commercial relationship with us.
3. WITH WHOM AND WHERE WILL WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?
We may share your personal information with various companies within our group.The Company may also share your personal information with the following third parties:

  1. Professional advisors such as auditors, accountants, and legal and financial consultants;
  2. Marketing and communications agencies that have agreed to process your personal data according to this Privacy Policy;
  3. Companies involved in market research;
  4. Our suppliers, business partners, and subcontractors;
  5. Search engines and web analytics providers.
We may disclose personal data to government authorities and/or law enforcement agencies if required for the purposes mentioned above, by law, or to protect the legal interests of the company, in compliance with applicable regulations. Personal data may also be shared with third-party service providers who process it on behalf of Linea Light Srl for the specified purposes.

These third parties include, but are not limited to, website hosting, maintenance, call center operations, and identity verification service providers.

If our business or a part of it is sold or merged with another company, your information will be disclosed to our advisors and the prospective buyer's advisors, and it will be transferred to the new owners of the business.
4. HOW LONG ARE MY PERSONAL DATA KEPT?
The Company will not hold onto your personal data longer than necessary for any given purpose, and we will only keep the specific information required for that purpose. Additionally, we must retain certain information as dictated by law and for as long as reasonably needed to meet legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud and abuse, and enforce our terms and conditions.

Since this data is collected at your request or with your explicit permission, we will store your information until you decide to stop receiving communications from us. You can do this by emailing info@influxbn.com or by using the "unsubscribe" link found in all our email communications. Once we receive your request to opt out, we will delete your information within three months.
5. HOW DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION SAFE?
We strive to safeguard your personal information with a range of organizational and technical security measures. We have put in place appropriate and reasonable protections to secure the personal data we handle. However, despite our precautions, no method of electronic transmission over the Internet or data storage can be completely secure. We cannot guarantee that hackers, cybercriminals, or other unauthorized entities will never breach our security measures and gain access to, steal, or alter your information. While we will do our utmost to protect your personal information, transmitting data to and from our Services carries some risk. You should only use the Services in a secure environment.
6. WHAT ARE MY RIGHTS IN RELATION TO MY PERSONAL DATA?
You have the right to request that we do not process your personal data for marketing purposes. You can prevent such processing by selecting certain options on the forms where we collect your data, clicking the "unsubscribe" button in any communication we send, or contacting us via email at info@influxbn.com.

If you have given your consent for the Company to use your personal data, you can withdraw it at any time. If the information we hold about you is incorrect or incomplete, you can inform us, and we will update it. Additionally, you have the right, subject to certain conditions and exceptions, to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. If your data has been provided by you and is processed electronically, you can request that it be delivered in a structured, machine-readable format. If you have a complaint about our processing of your personal data, you may ask us to limit its use until the issue is resolved.

In certain situations, you may also request the deletion of your personal data. This can be done if:
  1. You withdraw your consent for the Company to use your data;
  2. The data is no longer needed by the Company;
  3. You object to the use of your data, and the Company has no valid reason to continue using it;
  4. We have not handled your data in accordance with our obligations.
