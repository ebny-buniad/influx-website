LIGHTING AS ALL-ROUND-SERVICE-PACKAGE
While you take care of your core business, we'll take care of the rest. Our passion is to create lighting solutions through accomplished perfection. Our human-centric approach is our belief. In this way, we turn light into an experience
Each project phase requires the highest degree of attention. We will take care of it.
01 Analysis: at the beginning of a project it is all about the first inventory appraisal. At this early stage, our experts develop your requirements into an optimized lighting solution.
02 Planning: Once we understand your unique requirements, our team will develop a tailor made project plan including electrical planning, lighting design, lighting controls and emergency lighting, if desired we can also include digital services at this stage.
03 Coordination: We are on hand to offer expert advice and project support throughout the project life cycle
04 Finalization: Without having to pay upfront investment costs, you can profit from the latest lighting technology, installation and maintenance. All for a fixed monthly rate
05 Realisation: our expert commissioning and service technicians plan the lighting integration for your site, it is uncomplicated and tailor-made for you
06 Customer service: We are always by your side, you have a reliable partner throughout entire life cycle of the project!
In short: a holistic service portfolio for the entire lighting solution
